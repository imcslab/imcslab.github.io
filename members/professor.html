Prof. Seunghyun Park has been an Assistant Professor in the Division of Computer Engineering and the Director of the IMCS Lab at Hansung University since 2021. His research focuses on automotive cybersecurity, autonomous driving, information security and web engineering. Before joining Hansung University he spent more than a decade in industry, leading automotive security and cybersecurity crisis management at Hyundai Motor Company.
