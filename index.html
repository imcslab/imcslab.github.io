Hansung University · Division of Computer Engineering

Intelligent Mobility and Cybersecurity Lab.

The IMCS Lab conducts pioneering research in intelligent mobility and cybersecurity. Under the guidance of Prof. Seunghyun Park, we work on securing connected and autonomous vehicles — from in-vehicle intrusion detection and risk assessment methodology to EV charging authentication — creating solutions that impact both academia and industry.

Research themes Publications

Research topics

Our work spans automotive cybersecurity technology and regulation, and the fundamental technologies that make autonomous driving dependable.

Track 01

Automotive Cybersecurity Technology and Regulation

  • TARA (Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment) methods
  • CSMS (Cyber Security Management Systems) based on UN Regulation No. 155 and ISO/SAE 21434
  • Anomaly behavior and intrusion detection for in-vehicle networks
  • Plug and Charge based on ISO 15118
  • Blockchain and smart contracts for vehicle software

Track 02

Fundamental Technology for Autonomous Vehicles

  • Recognizing moving objects from multiple sensing data fusion
  • Trajectory planning for collision avoidance
  • Automotive software update Over-The-Air (Automotive OTA)
